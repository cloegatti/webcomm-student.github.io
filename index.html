About Me

Born in Ukraine and raised between Bologna and Modena, I grew up exploring the Romagnolo countryside. I love living in the moment and capturing the beauty I encounter in Italy, especially in Emilia Romagna, where my heart belongs, as much as I adore Spain. Working as a gardener allows me to combine my two passions: nature and photography, capturing stunning flowers every day. I am available for any nature-related project, as well as event photography, including weddings and special occasions.
